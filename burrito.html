Recipe for a Carne Asada Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs Skirt or flank steak

    • For the Marinade:

  • 1/4 Cup oragne juice,
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 3 Minced garlic cloves
  • 1 Tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 Tablespoon cumin
  • Salt to taste

    • Fillings:

  • 2 Cups cooked mexican rice or cilantro lime rice
  • 1 Can (15 oz) pinto or refried beans, warmed
  • 2 Cups shredded cheese (Monterey Jack, Cheddar, or Oaxaca)
  • Fresh guacamole
  • Pico de gallo or tomatillo salsa
  • Chopped fresh cilantro and white onion

Marinate the Steak

Combine the steak and marinade ingredients in a ziplock bag and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (up to 8 hours).

Cook the Meat

Heat a grill or cast-iron skillet to high heat. Cook the steak for about 3-5 minutes per side until nicely charred and medium-rare (internal temp of 135° F to 145° F). Let it rest for 10 minutes, then dice it against the grain into bite-sized cubes.

Warm the Tortillas

Place tortillas in a dry skillet over medium heat for about 30 seconds per side until soft and pliable.

Assemble the Burrito

  • Lay a tortilla flat. Spread a base of refried beans, followed by rice, carne asada, cheese, guacamole, and Pico de Gallo
  • Fold the two opposite sides of the tortilla inward. While holding the sides tight, pull the bottom flap up and over the filling, tuck tightly, and roll forward.

Toast the Burrito

Place the rolled burrito seam-side down in a hot, dry skillet. Cook for 1-2 minutes per side until the tortilla is crispy and the cheese inside has fully melted.
